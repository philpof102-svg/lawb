LAWB mirror — claim

This is the airdrop claim page for the non-official LAWB mirror. Sign in with the wallet you want the airdrop sent to. The signature stays in your browser — we only see your wallet address, your X handle, and the tweet you engaged with. Read the source: it's a single static HTML file.

How it works: we ask your wallet to sign a fixed message. From the signature we recover your address; that address is the one we airdrop to. The signature never leaves your browser. If your wallet is not installed, we cannot airdrop to you.
